Services

Cleaning

Whether you require a one-time green-to-clean scouring or ongoing monthly service, Perfect Blue has a plan to fit your needs and budget. Your pool should be the centerpiece of the back yard. We can make it look fun and inviting again.

Repair

A pool that doesn't work right is no fun for anyone. Fortunately, cracked foundations and leaks don't stand a chance against our staff of trained, insured pool repair experts. We can provide an estimate with one quick trip to your location.

Renovation

Ready for a different look? Let us advise you on where to take your pool in order to reach its maximum potential for beauty and entertainment.

Efficiency

Many of our clients seem to have a common problem: they're paying too much for electricity each month. You might be surprised by how inefficient an older pool pump can be, and how much you can save by installing a more efficient one. Using a watt meter, we can calculate exactly how much energy your pump uses, and how much you could be saving each month by replacing it.
